2020 was a difficult year for many people, and for obvious reasons: The way to conduct business was turned completely on its head, with many working from home, and the rest of us forced to cover our faces with masks all day.

I work both in education as a lecturer and in hospitality, so I felt this impact pretty strongly. Like many others, I began to examine my professional goals more closely.

I have always enjoyed programming and working with datasets. As such, over the last 18 months and outside of my full time job, I have focused on expanding my knowledge in this area. I've taken courses in Business Analytics, SQL, Tableau, R and R-Studio, collaborated on web and android applications that use object-oriented programming languages such as Python and Java, explored machine learning on Kaggle, and, most recently, I have completed the Google Data Analytics Specialisation on Coursera.

I am deeply interested in the data analytics field and dedicate my spare time to learning as much on the topic as I can. In addition, I retain my interest in app and web development and continue to work in this area as well. This site is a demonstration of my interests and a record of what I've learned so far. I am planning here to explore my other interests in sustainability,tourism, hospitality, and education, so in the end I hope to assemble an interesting collection of projects.
